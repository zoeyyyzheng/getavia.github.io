Our Story

How three Stanford students turned a class project into a revolutionary aviation AI company

The Beginning

We met in Stanford's CS224G class, where we immediately connected over our shared passion for AI and aviation. On the first day of class, Rana pitched her idea, sparking a conversation that led to deeper discussions about the challenges in aviation training and safety.

From there, Rana and Zoey interviewed several software developers to find the right technical partner, and we found that we clicked best with Xiaoyue. Our skills and vision aligned perfectly, and we quickly realized the massive potential in bringing AI copiloting into aviation.

All founders have met in person, and we have been working closely together ever since.

Technical Excellence

All technical work, including coding, AI model development, and system integration, is done by our co-founders Rana Taki and Xiaoyue Wang. They have been responsible for:

  • Building Avia's speech-to-text and text-to-speech systems
  • Fine-tuning the LLM for aviation guidance
  • Integrating hardware such as Garmin SD cards and Bose headsets
